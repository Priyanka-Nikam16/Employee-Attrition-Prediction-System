This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition patterns within an organization. The goal is to identify key factors influencing attrition, build predictive models, and provide actionable insights to improve employee retention.

Objectives

Understand the distribution of employee salaries, roles, and demographics.
Identify correlations between attrition and factors such as salary, tenure, job role, and performance.
Develop predictive models to estimate attrition risk.
Provide HR teams with data-driven recommendations for retention strategies.
